Armenian to English Speech Translator

Record your voice in Armenian, and this application will translate it into English and return the translated speech as audio. You can record up to 30 seconds of audio per request.

There are three models to choose from for the output English speech: Fast Pitch offers fast and high-quality synthesis, Your TTS supports speaker adaptation, it will speak in your voice, and Bark allows selecting between male and female voices.
